Namecheap FreeDNS offers free DNS hosting with an easy-to-use interface — ideal for Namecheap customers.

<Note>

## Quick setup

1. Create a Namecheap account.
2. Open the FreeDNS service.
3. Add your subdomain.
4. Note your Namecheap nameservers.
5. Register with Stackryze Domains using your nameservers.

For detailed instructions, check back soon, join the Discord community, or contact support.
