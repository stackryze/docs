deSEC is a free, privacy-focused DNS service with built-in DNSSEC — a good fit for security-conscious users.

<Note>
  This guide is in progress. For the most up-to-date instructions, join the [Discord community](https://discord.gg/wr7s97cfM7) or email [support@stackryze.com](mailto:support@stackryze.com).
</Note>

## Quick setup

1. Create a deSEC account at [desec.io](https://desec.io).
2. Create a domain for your subdomain.
3. Note your deSEC nameservers.
4. Register with Stackryze Domains using your nameservers.

For detailed instructions, check back soon, join the Discord community, or contact support.
