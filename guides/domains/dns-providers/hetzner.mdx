Hetzner DNS is a free DNS service from Hetzner with reliable infrastructure and a simple API.

<Note>
  This guide is in progress. For the most up-to-date instructions, join the [Discord community](https://discord.gg/wr7s97cfM7) or email [support@stackryze.com](mailto:support@stackryze.com).
</Note>

## Quick setup

1. Create a Hetzner account.
2. Open the Hetzner DNS Console.
3. Create a zone for your subdomain.
4. Note your Hetzner nameservers.
5. Register with Stackryze Domains using your nameservers.

For detailed instructions, check back soon, join the Discord community, or contact support.
