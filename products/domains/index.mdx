Stackryze Domains gives you a real, public subdomain you can use on any project, portfolio, or side hustle. No credit card, no renewal fees, no surprise captures.

<Note>
  The full service lives at [domain.stackryze.com](https://domain.stackryze.com). This site documents how to register, manage, and connect a Stackryze Domain to your DNS provider.
</Note>

## Get started in four steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign in at [domain.stackryze.com](https://domain.stackryze.com). Email or GitHub both work.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Register a subdomain">
    Pick an `indevs.in` name (3–63 characters, lowercase letters, numbers, hyphens) and complete the CAPTCHA.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your DNS provider">
    Keep the default Stackryze nameservers, or point your subdomain at any provider you prefer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your records">
    Open the provider's dashboard and add the records you need. We document the full flow for [Cloudflare](/products/domains/dns-providers/cloudflare), [Route 53](/products/domains/dns-providers/route53), [DigitalOcean](/products/domains/dns-providers/digitalocean), and more.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What's included

Every Stackryze Domain includes, out of the box:

| Feature | Included |
| --- | --- |
| A public subdomain (`yourname.indevs.in`) | ✅ |
| Editable DNS zone pointing at Stackryze nameservers | ✅ |
| Email forwarding (`you@yourname.indevs.in`) | ✅ |
| Wildcard records (`*.yourname.indevs.in`) | ✅ |
| Custom nameservers (BYOD) | ✅ |
| 1-year registration, renewable | ✅ |
