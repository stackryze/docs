Stackryze Hosting is the next product in the lineup. The same four pillars as DNS and Domains: trustworthy, affordable, independent, and open by default.

<Note>
  Hosting is currently in planning. The full service will live at `hosting.stackryze.com` when it ships.
</Note>

## What to expect

- **Affordable by default.** Flat, upfront pricing — no bandwidth surprises, no renewal traps.
- **Open-source runtime.** The hosting platform is built on open software and will ship with a public roadmap.
- **Free tier for everyone.** A usable free plan, the same way DNS and Domains do it today.
- **First-class DNS.** Every hosted project ships with a Stackryze DNS zone, pre-wired.
