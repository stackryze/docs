## Hosting release history

Stackryze Hosting is moving from internal alpha to a closed beta over the summer of 2026. Every release is documented below.

### 2026

| Version | Date | Highlights |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [v0.3.0](/changelog/hosting/v0.3.0) | July 1, 2026 | Closed beta — free tier, preview environments per PR, env vars with secret scanning, public roadmap. |
| [v0.2.0](/changelog/hosting/v0.2.0) | June 15, 2026 | Private beta — custom domains via Stackryze DNS, automatic HTTPS via ACME DNS-01, Git-based deploys. |
| [v0.1.0](/changelog/hosting/v0.1.0) | May 30, 2026 | Internal alpha — container deploy on `stackryze.run` subdomains, automatic health-check rollback. |
